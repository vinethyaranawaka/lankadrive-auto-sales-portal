LankaDrive Auto Sales (Pvt) Ltd. is a dynamic and customer-focused automotive trading company based in Colombo, committed to delivering reliable, high-quality vehicles and exceptional service to customers across Sri Lanka.


Founded with a vision to simplify the vehicle buying and selling experience, LankaDrive Auto Sales specializes in sourcing a wide range of vehicles, including passenger cars, SUVs, and light commercial vehicles. The company focuses on connecting customers with dependable mobility solutions that match their lifestyle, budget, and expectations.At LankaDrive, trust is the engine that drives every transaction. The company emphasizes transparency, fair pricing, and professional guidance, ensuring that customers make informed decisions with confidence. From vehicle selection to documentation support, every step is handled with care and efficiency.


With the Sri Lankan automotive industry evolving rapidly driven by digital marketplaces and customer demand for convenience, LankaDrive Auto Sales aims to stay ahead by combining traditional business values with modern service approaches. The company continues to build long-term relationships with individuals, businesses, and automotive partners through reliability and consistent performance.
